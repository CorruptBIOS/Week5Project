Common Exercises

The following are common exercises that we encourage our clients to as part of their daily exercise routine.

Burpee
Burpees are a great, full body exercise to increase your strength and endurance. Begin in a standing position, drop into a squat and extend your hands forward, kick your feet back and then forward again quickly, and then jump up from a squattted position.
Plank
Planks build your core strength. To perform a plank, get in a push up position and rest your forearms on the floor. Hold the position as long as you can.
Mountain Climber
Mountain Climbers are a good cardio exercise. Place your hands on the floor in a push up position, then bring on knee up to your waist and then switch to another knee as quickly as you can (as though you are climbing a mountain)
